# SIE Style Preset (Fixed)

## Palette

- Brand primary: `#AD053D`
- Brand secondary: `#932341`
- Light pink accent: `#EFDDE2`
- Title text: `#2C3E50`
- Body text: `#4A5558`
- Auxiliary text: `#6B7E85`
- Divider line: `#7C969D`
- Border/Icon: `#B4C6CA`
- Cover bg: `#2C3E50`
- Content bg: `#F2F6F6`
- Card bg: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography

- Title: `Microsoft YaHei` Bold
- Body: `Microsoft YaHei Light`
- Notes/caption: `Microsoft YaHei`

## Density and rhythm

- Cover/section: 1-2 large visual elements, high contrast
- Content pages: 3-5 modules, line-based structure
- Data pages: table-first, chart-second
- 4-6 points max per page

## Data semantics

- Positive: `#2D8B4E`
- Negative: `#C41E3A`

## Forbidden

- Inter / Roboto / Arial
- Center-align everything
- Decorative glassmorphism / heavy shadow
- `#6366f1` generic indigo style
